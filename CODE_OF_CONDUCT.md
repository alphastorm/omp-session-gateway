# Community code of conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for contributors and users regardless of background, identity, experience level, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and patient.
- Critique designs and code rather than people.
- Assume good intent while still addressing security and correctness directly.
- Respect privacy and never disclose another person's collaboration link, transcript, identity, path, or diagnostic data.
- Accept maintainer decisions and documented scope boundaries even when proposing alternatives.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, doxxing, sustained disruption, personal attacks, deliberate secret disclosure, and attempts to pressure maintainers into unsafe publication are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer or the contact listed in the repository's security/community settings. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close or lock discussions, reject contributions, or temporarily/permanently restrict participation based on severity and pattern.

Reports will be handled as privately as practical. Retaliation against a reporter is prohibited.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, code review, project chat, release coordination, and public spaces where someone represents the project.
